San Luis Obispo's #1 Cat Cafe!

Open 7AM-4PM daily

The Magic Cat is a coffee shop that allows adoptable cats to mingle with customers in hopes of these kitties finding a forever home. Customers are offered drinks, baked goods, and about 10-15 friendly kitties to hang out with during their stay. The amount of kitties in need of a loving home is overwhelming, so by introducing cats to their customers, The Magic Cat hopes to reach more adopters.
